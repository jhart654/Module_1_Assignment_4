Hov National Park

With our history and lots of opportunities for fun and recreation, there's no wonder why this park is often called “America's best idea.” Generations of visitors have explored the parks and come away with a deep appreciation for these special places.

Why Us?

Hiking Trails

Hov National Park features 15 miles of hiking and walking trails with varying degrees of difficulty. Enjoy the 5-mile trail around the base, the 1-mile trail to the summit and more!

Visitor Center

Whether you need a bottle of water or some postcards, you’ll find places to buy them in here at our Visitor Center.

Park Rangers

Our park rangers wear many hats, serving as guides, educators and protectors. Our rangers know the Hov National Park like the backs of their hands. They are some of the best people to ask for hiking and sightseeing recommendations.
